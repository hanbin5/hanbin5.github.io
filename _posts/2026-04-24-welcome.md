Hi, I'm **Hanbin**. I'm an undergraduate at Chung-Ang University, and this is my research blog / portfolio site.

## Why I built this

I realized I've been learning a lot from reading other people's research notes and project write-ups — but I wasn't writing any myself. Reading is passive; writing forces you to notice the parts you only *thought* you understood. So this site is an attempt to change that.

## What to expect

Roughly three kinds of posts:

1. **Paper reading notes** — when I read a paper closely, I'll try to write up what I learned, usually in the form "here's the setup, here's the key insight, here's the part I didn't follow on the first pass."
2. **Project write-ups** — reproductions, side projects, tooling. The goal isn't to show off working code (that's what GitHub is for); it's to share the *thinking* that led there and the mistakes along the way.
3. **Random thoughts** — half-baked ideas I want to remember. Usually short.

## Topics

Mostly **diffusion models** and **robotics**, since that's what I'm thinking about these days. Sometimes ML fundamentals that I feel shaky on. Occasionally programming tools or linux things.
