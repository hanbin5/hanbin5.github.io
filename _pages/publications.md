## How to add a paper (instructions for future-me)

Each paper goes under a year heading in the format below. Pick the style you prefer when the first one lands — I'd recommend keeping it short and consistent.

### Template

```
### <Year>

**<Paper title>**
<Authors (bold yourself)>
*<Venue>*, <Year>
[[PDF](link)] [[arXiv](link)] [[code](link)] [[bibtex](link)]

One-sentence TL;DR.
```
