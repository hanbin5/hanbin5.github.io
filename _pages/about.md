## Research interests

My interests sit at the intersection of **diffusion models** and **robotics** — I'm curious about how generative models can learn rich policies and world models that let robots act robustly in the messy physical world. Concretely:

- **Diffusion policies** for manipulation and locomotion — why modeling the *action distribution* (not its mean) helps on multimodal demonstrations.
- **World models** that combine learned dynamics with generative priors — for planning, imagination, and sample-efficient control.
- **Imitation learning and offline RL** with diffusion-based representations.
